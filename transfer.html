      Drag & Drop Transfer

      StoAmigo makes transferring files between machines a breeze. No cables or thumb drives. Just drag and drop folders across devices – no matter what and where they are. Freely move files to and from your work and home computers. Clear up valuable phone memory by sending the pictures and videos you take straight to your desktop.

      Move Anything

      No arbitrary size limits. Add entire hard drives into your ecosystem – letting you manage where you put your files with complete flexibility. Backup large files, folders, and even whole devices with ease. Copy your phone or laptop content directly onto your desktop – for convenient access and added security.

      Connect Your Entire Ecosystem

      StoAmigo's unique tacking feature lets you link together all your devices. It’s the only solution that lets you easily move files between desktop, phone, and tablet - without any uploading. Everything is direct, device-to-device. Access anything from anywhere, move files to where they actually belong, and do it all with a few clicks.
