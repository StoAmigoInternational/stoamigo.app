      Hey, we're StoAmigo

      Files shouldn't be something you have to think about.

      You should control what happens with your files. It should be easy.

      These two beliefs are what drove us to build AXEL (formerly StoAmigo) - the simpler way to access and manage your files from any device.

      Unlike other solutions, it's direct. Files move or stream from one device straight to another. No stops along the way. No uploading. No middlemen.

      Available for Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, and Web - over two million people have used the AXEL App to make files easy.

      The future is decentralized.

      We're helping make it happen.


      Founded in 2012, we've always been about giving people full control over their digital lives in a world that's increasingly cavalier about taking it away. The first realization of our vision was the Indiegogo-funded CloudLocker, a physical, private cloud storage device that delivered all the power of the cloud without the need to upload anything anywhere else.

      StoAmigo was the natural “next step” of that technology – providing even greater functionality without the external hardware.

      In 2018, we rebranded into AXEL. It signaled an evolution of our vision, a new focus on utilizing blockchain technology to give people the control not only over their files – but their personal data too. All without sacrificing convenience.

      We're not done yet.

      Based in Las Vegas, we have team members located all over North America, Europe, and Asia. Our technology has been featured in Forbes, Popular Mechanics, and PCMag.

      AXEL is our flagship app, built off of our patented TackApp technology that enables unification without centralization.

      We're constantly working to evolve AXEL into the one file app that does absolutely everything you could want. Effortlessly.
