        Your Files, Your Way

        Control everything that happens to your files. Where they go. How they are stored. Who can access them and who can't. StoAmigo doesn't force you into an online file ecosystem like other services. Choose exactly where you put your data and what happens to it.

        Flexibility Through Choice

        Dynamic storage provides near-limitless options on where and how you store your data. Share files from your own drives without size restrictions. Or put them in your free 2 GB cloud storage to make them available 24/7.

        Setup < 1 Minute

        Connect (or "tack") a folder or drive on any machine to your StoAmigo account to access it remotely. Connected folders are immediately accessible through our web-based interface or mobile app; making it easy to view, download, and share files. Easily stream videos to your phone or access your work documents from home.
