      Watch Anything, Anywhere

      Have a video on your desktop that you want to watch on other devices? Simply access that file or folder through StoAmigo and that video will be streamed directly to your phone, laptop, or tablet. Whether it's a feature-length movie or a one minute video clip – your content is instantly available to watch from anywhere.

      Share the Moments

      No uploading and no waiting when sending videos to family, friends, and colleagues. Wedding tapes, feature-length films, marketing testimonials – make anything with a play and pause button available to anyone. Simply right click on a video to send a link, which can then be shared with other viewers for easy access.

      Plug & Play

      Videos you share with StoAmigo can be watched by anyone without downloading anything. No third-party software is required to receive the streaming video – not even StoAmigo. Whether you share a cartoon, movie, or media clip, that file is played by StoAmigo's built-in media player on the other end. Share videos with anyone, instantly, on any device.
